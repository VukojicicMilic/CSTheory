General recommendations

  • Say NO to proprietary JS!
  • Use LibreJS
  • Surfing on the internet can be a pleasant thing. But please be safe! JShelter | HTTPS Everywhere | Privacy Badger
  • Use FREE software in education.
  • Communication without Google Meet, Zoom, Skype, Viber, Whatsapp, Telegram… Safe and ethical alternatives: Jitsi and Jami
  • Encrypt and sign your data and communications GnuPG | Easy setup for Windows users: Gpg4win
  • Operating systems that respects your FREEDOM: GNU/Linux distros | Easy to use: Trisquel
