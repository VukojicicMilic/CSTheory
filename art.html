About logo 

The logo on the site was created by artificial intelligence and represents a mix of three animals: Grimpoteuthis (dumbo octopuse), Owl and Penguin.

Why:

  • Octopus symbolizes  wisdom, intelligence, and complexity.
  • Owl symbolizes wealth, prosperity, wisdom, good luck, and fortune.
  • Penguin symbolizes dreams, adaptability, dual-natures, closeness, family, and spirituality (and Linux :) ).

Also, I have to give a special place to the platypus and wolf , which is one of my favorite animals, platypus symbolizes creativity, uniqueness and self-acceptance and wolf symbolizes loyalty, family, communication, wisdom, teaching, and intelligence.
